<p>One night in Ogonyo

<p>Over the past two decades, Uganda's civil war has forced hundreds of thousands of families into miserable camps. Now, buoyed by recent peace talks, many are clustering into less-cramped "return camps."

<p>Senior writer Roger Burks spent a night alongside displaced families in one of these makeshift villages, Ogonyo, where Mercy Corps is helping residents support themselves while they await the day they can return home.
