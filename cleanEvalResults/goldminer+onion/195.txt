<h>Welcome

<p>The Center for Instruction, Research, and Technology explores,
 develops, promotes, and supports effective teaching and research
 practices to advance knowledge and active learning at Indiana
 State University. CIRT endeavors to have a measurable impact
 on the academic community by building the reputation of Indiana
 State for innovative instruction and technology-enhanced research.

<p>The Center is under the domain of the Associate Vice President
 for Academic Affairs and Chief Information Officer and has strong
 ties to the Provost's and is guided by a faculty advisory committee.
