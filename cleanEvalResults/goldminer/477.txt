<p>How to stop the £14 billion workplace injury drain

 seven steps employers should take to prevent workplace injuries

 injury and ill-health, according to TUC research. Risks 64,

<p>What it costs to hurt you 
 The European Agency for Safety and
 Health at Work has produced a new tool to "improve occupational
 safety and health and to increase the competitiveness of your
 business." It allows you to calculate the additional cash flow
 that would be generated if the firm invested in specific preventive
 measures - a standard "cost-benefit' analysis." European Agency
 news release Inventory of socio-economic costs of work accidents.
